Welcome Future and Current Scholars!

We're thrilled to have you checking this page out as this page contains WORLD CLASS SONNETS from past students. The poems are about life and our experiences of growing up and navigating through its challenges. Each poem serves as a glimpse into various aspects of our personal journeys and highlights the emotions, struggles, and experiences that we have encountered along the way. Through our poems, we hope to convey our understanding of life and its hurdles, as well as the valuable lessons and takeaways that we have learned from them. Here are 6 Sonnets from 3 different students. These Sonnets are both Petrarchan and Shakesperean!

' THEME: Life's Journey '

My Life: Growing Up

Getting Older by Billie Eilish
Sonnet by Sean Naval

What’s the purpose of embracing my youth?
If we all just depart from this world
The burden on me is unfurled
I’m getting older, now I shall embrace the sooth

This intricate labyrinth of a land bestow
While the ray of the sun climbs
The darkness, all alone, resigns
Each second of this life shouldn’t end with a sorrow

I'm becoming more adept at owning up to my mistakes
Oh, let us follow the sounds that are to be found
Yet there are challenges that can make me break

Ink starts to write, A story makes
I’m happier than ever, my goal sounds
The end of a beginning, now a new one awakes

HER.

my future by Billie Eilish
Sonnet by Sean Naval

Upon this land, Im in love
But not to anyone, its with her, my future
It might be as beautiful as a dove
But it also might be as ugly as a sewer

Though the challenges upon this empire
I can’t wait to meet her
She could be bold and the umpire
She might also be a bit of a blur

Thou shalt hear a melody
A melody that makes her dance
Each move, each dance, a memory
Appears like a trance

Once more, I'm in love with what lies from this day forward
I just want to know if she will become the standard

Path Unfolded

Top of my School by Katherine Lynn-Rose
Sonnet by Brenice David

In life, some people drift, accepting what’s bestowed
While others strive to make their own dreams touch the sky.
Their unyielding hearts, aspirations soaring high,
Yet others hesitate, on paths they’ve never strolled.

To go all out, every task I must unload
Outdoing all the rest, where no doubts arise high
Me? Settling for second best? I must testify
I won’t stop and lose my cool, for the dreams I owed.

And who will cherish me if not the chosen spark?
Even if not the top, I’m still nobody’s fool
And all these years, I’ve been labeled as overzealous
Struggling way too much, making everything dark.
But as I shine like a star with passion as my tool,
Let people see, in their hearts, a hint of jealous.

A Journey Through Shadows and Lights

Fix You by Coldplay
Sonnet by Brenice David

Giving your utmost best but not rewarded,
Fulfilled desires, while needs are to no success
Exhausted, yet sleep’s recognition thwarted
Tears cascading, with pain that cease to express.

Things once embraced, now unable to replace
A bitter fate, with love fading out of view
Everything is worsening, with no escape
In the dark shadows deep, where memories flew.

In the midst of chaos, hope’s light finds your path
Guiding through darkness, evoking your breakthrough
And one caresses the wounded aftermath
which afterwards, whispers, very gentle and truev
It murmured, if you don’t try, you’ll never know,
Discover your worth, let your spirits grow.

FINDING YOUR VOICE

LETTER TO MY 13 YEAR OLD SELF by LAUFEY
Sonnet by Yesha Esguerra

I know you stand alone, so different from
the crowd
Your eyes filled with fear, your heart, so
unsure
But, baby, just know you'll grow up and
grow so tough
And charm those in your sight
For you are a bright light
Don't be afraid to show it
Love and be loved
Is all that you need
And no one else's voice
Can ever drown yours out

SEEKING SALVATION

YOU'RE ON YOUR OWN KID by Taylor Swift
Sonnet by Yesha Esguerra

She believed in her beauty and her worth
But all around her, she saw others and their
strength
And her heart would ache, for she felt so
frail
And though her light never faded, it dimmed
sometimes
Amid all the noise and chaos in life
Like in a storm, her spirit would take flight
But through it all, she kept on trying
To find her place in the world and to make it
her own
And finally, she found what she was seeking
Love and appreciation that never left her
alone

Sonnets made by
SEAN NAVAL,
BRENICE DAVID,
AND YESHA ESGUERRA
